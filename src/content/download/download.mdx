### Available Platforms

The app is currently available on the official app stores for:

- **macOS =>** [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/it/app/eyerest-comfort-vision/id6476984614)
- **Windows =>** [Microsoft Store](https://apps.microsoft.com/detail/9p09whdtwd93)
- **Future support for Linux** is planned.

### Requirements

- The app does not require an internet connection to function.
- However, it does require a connected camera to operate.
